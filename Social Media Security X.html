Social Media Security

Stay Safe Online: Protect Your Privacy and Personal Information

Key Tips for Social Media Security

1. Use Strong Privacy Settings

Ensure your social media profiles are private, and only share your information with people you trust.

2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Activate two-factor authentication to add an extra layer of security to your social media accounts.

3. Use Strong and Unique Passwords

Create strong, unique passwords for each of your social media accounts to prevent unauthorized access.

4. Avoid Public Wi-Fi for Sensitive Actions

Avoid accessing your social media accounts on public Wi-Fi networks, as they can be less secure.

5. Report Suspicious Activity

Report any suspicious activity or messages on your social media accounts to the platform administrators.
