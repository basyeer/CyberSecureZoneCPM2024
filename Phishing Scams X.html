Avoiding Phishing Scams

Stay Safe Online: Know the Signs, Protect Yourself

Key Tips to Avoid Phishing Scams

1. Think Before You Click

Always double-check links in emails or texts before clicking. Hover over the link to preview the URL, ensuring it leads to a trusted website.

2. Verify the Source

If you receive an unexpected email from a financial institution or service provider, contact them directly using official channels to confirm the authenticity.

3. Beware of Urgency

Phishing scams often create a sense of urgency to trick you into immediate action. Take a step back and analyze the situation before responding.

4. Use Multi-Factor Authentication

Enable multi-factor authentication on your accounts for an extra layer of security, reducing the chances of unauthorized access.

5. Keep Software Updated

Regularly update your devices and software to protect against known vulnerabilities that attackers might exploit.
